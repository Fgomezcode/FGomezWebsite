SELECT

Make a closed hand while in a sector to lock in your answer.

NAVIGATE

Move your open hand to navigate between sectors.

SHOW ANSWER

Two open hands will reveal the answer.

SKIP QUESTION

Two closed hands will skip the question.

QUIT GAME

Two upward pointing hands will quit the game.

PLAY MUSIC

One closed and one open hand will play the music if it is stopped.

STOP MUSIC

One closed and one open hand will pause the music.
